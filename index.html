Biography

I am a Ph.D. student at KAIST Data Intelligence Lab, advised by Professor Steven Euijong Whang. My research interests center on Data Augmentation for Responsible AI and Data-centric AI, where I explore methods to improve the robustness of AI systems while enhancing data quality and diversity. I have developed deep expertise in data augmentation techniques that are crucial for enhancing AI model performance and mitigating overfitting.

My research contributions span multiple domains including AI, Computer Vision, and Data Mining. Through collaborations with Samsung Electronics and Samsung Research, I have gained extensive industry experience implementing AI solutions that address real-world challenges, effectively bridging the gap between theoretical research and practical applications.
